Meet Your Host

Hi, I'm Francisca.

Bread baker, mother, and teacher.

I'm a mother of four living in northern Portugal, and I believe bread connects us to what matters most — our families, our traditions, and the planet we share.

I started baking sourdough in 2015 and quickly realised the problem wasn't the recipes — it was the lack of method. Once I understood the process, everything changed. Now I teach families to do the same: bake bread they're proud of, with less waste and more joy.
