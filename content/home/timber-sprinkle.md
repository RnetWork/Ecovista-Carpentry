# Home Page Addition: Timber Value Sprinkle

Two new pieces of content for the Home page, both pulling from the Journal
article "Why Timber Frame Homes Are the Future." Hyphen free. Placement
guidance included for Claude Code, matching the structure laid out in
section 6 of ECOVISTA_BRIEF.md.

---

## 1. Intro statement addition

Use as the brand statement block (Home page, section 2 in the brief: "Intro
statement", large type, lots of whitespace). Replaces or extends whatever
brand statement copy is used there.

> We build timber frame homes that work as hard for the planet as they do
> for the people living in them. Every cubic metre of timber we use stores
> roughly 0.9 tonnes of CO₂, while cutting energy bills by up to 30%
> compared to traditional construction. Modern architecture, built from a
> material that gives back more than it takes.

(Short, large type, sits comfortably under your existing hero. Pulls the two
strongest numbers from the Journal piece into the very first thing a visitor
reads about what Eco Vista actually builds.)
